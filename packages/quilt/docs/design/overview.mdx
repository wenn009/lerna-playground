# Design Overview

When designing and choosing components to build your UI with, here are two questions to start with:

1. What goal am I trying to achieve?
2. Does this component I’m thinking of using help me achieve that goal?

Knowing a component’s purpose helps clarify whether it helps you reach your goal(s). The DNA behind a majority of Quilt components can be distilled into three purposes, or principles briefly explained below. Refer to the individual sections in the left side panel for more detailed and thorough information.

Something you have to interact with (or simply, things where you have to use your **mouse and/or keyboard**).

## Information

Something you use your **eyeballs** to consume and your brain to comprehend. You can use your mouse for additional comprehension (think tooltips), but other than that these are mainly for your eyes to consume.
